# Tomatito Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-05-02_

Tomatito is built around one simple promise: nothing leaves your device.

## What we collect

Nothing.

## What is stored on your device

- Your timer settings (focus / break durations, theme, sound choice, daily goal).
- A local history of completed focus periods, used for the streak counter and the weekly bar chart.
- Optional: a rolling local crash log (max 1 MB), used only when you tap "Send crash log" in About to compose a pre-filled email to the maintainer. The log never leaves your device unless you send it yourself.

This data lives in your app's private storage and is removed when you uninstall Tomatito.

## What we do not do

- No analytics SDKs.
- No crash-reporting SDKs.
- No advertising SDKs.
- No accounts.
- No cloud sync.
- No network calls at runtime. Tomatito does not request the INTERNET permission on Android.

## Permissions

| Permission | Why |
|-----------|-----|
| Notifications (Android, opt-in) | Persistent timer notification and end-of-period chime. |
| Foreground service (Android) | Keep the timer accurate when the screen is off, while the persistent notification is enabled. |
| Vibrate (Android, opt-in) | Optional vibration on chime. |

We never request battery optimisation exemption automatically.

## Children

Tomatito is not directed at children but is suitable for general audiences (IARC rating: 3+).

## Changes

When this policy changes, the app About screen surfaces a one-time notice with a diff link.
